Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our chatbot application ("Service", "App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our AI-powered, rule-based, and hybrid chatbot services. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • Account means a unique account created for you to access our Service
  • Application refers to our chatbot application, including AI chatbot, rule-based chatbot, and hybrid chatbot features
  • Company (referred to as "We," "Us," or "Our") refers to the chatbot application provider
  • Device means any device that can access the Service, such as a computer, smartphone, or tablet
  • Personal Data means any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual
  • Service refers to the chatbot Application
  • Usage Data refers to data collected automatically, either generated by use of the Service or from the Service infrastructure itself
  • Conversation Data refers to the messages, queries, and responses exchanged between you and our chatbots
  • AI Chatbot refers to our artificial intelligence-powered conversational interface
  • Rule-Based Chatbot refers to our pre-programmed response system
  • Hybrid Chatbot refers to our combined AI and rule-based chatbot system

Information We Collect

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • User preferences and settings
  • Profile information

Conversation Data

We collect and store:

  • Messages and queries you send to our chatbots
  • Responses provided by our AI, rule-based, and hybrid chatbots
  • Conversation history and context
  • Feedback and ratings you provide on chatbot responses
  • Language preferences

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service and may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages of our Service that you visit
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Time spent on those pages
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Diagnostic data
  • Chatbot interaction patterns
  • Feature usage statistics

AI Training Data

For our AI Chatbot features:

  • Conversations may be analyzed to improve AI model performance
  • Anonymized conversation data may be used for training purposes
  • User feedback helps refine AI responses

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including all chatbot functionalities
  • To manage your Account and provide access to different chatbot features
  • To improve our chatbots, including enhancing AI accuracy, rule-based responses, and hybrid performance
  • To personalize your experience by remembering your preferences and conversation history
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To send notifications about Service updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize chatbot performance
  • To train and improve our AI models (using anonymized data)
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or fraudulent activity
  • For compliance with legal obligations

AI-Specific Considerations

  • Our AI chatbot may occasionally provide inaccurate or incomplete information
  • AI responses are generated based on training data and may not reflect current events
  • We do not guarantee the accuracy of AI-generated content
  • Critical decisions should not be made solely based on chatbot responses

Human Review

  • Some conversations may be reviewed by human moderators for quality assurance
  • Personal identifiers are removed where possible during review
  • You can opt out of human review by contacting us

Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers who help us operate and improve our chatbots
  • With AI service providers that power our AI chatbot features
  • For business transfers in connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your Personal Data and Conversation Data only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy:

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data, including:
